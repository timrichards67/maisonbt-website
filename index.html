Family Budget

Your family’s money, made clear.

Every bank account and credit card in one clear view—with zero password sharing. Stop logging into multiple apps just to see where you stand. Family Budget brings everything together on your iPhone and iPad with a native experience designed for your household.

Instead of sharing your bank passwords, simply drop in your downloaded statements. Our smart wizard learns your bank’s layout in seconds, keeping your credentials private and your bank's security intact. We then handle the heavy lifting—automatically sorting your spending into Essentials, Priorities, and Fun Stuff.
